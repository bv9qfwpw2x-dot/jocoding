Lotto Number Generator

About Lotto 6/45

Lotto 6/45 is a popular lottery game where participants choose six numbers from a pool of 1 to 45. Our generator helps you pick numbers using a fair and random algorithm, ensuring each selection is unique.

How to Play

To play, simply select six numbers. If all six match the official draw, you win the jackpot! Even if you match fewer numbers, there are various prize tiers available.

Winning Strategies & Tips

Odd/Even Balance

A common strategy is to balance odd and even numbers. Many winning draws have a 3/3 or 2/4 split between odd and even digits.

High/Low Spread

Try to spread your numbers across the low (1-22) and high (23-45) ranges for a more statistically common distribution.
